Contact

The door is open.

Teachers, parents, students, engineers, librarians — if you'd like a demo day, a tutoring match, a partnership, or simply to ask a question, write to youngmedicalengineers@gmail.com or find us on Instagram @ym_engineers.

Is tutoring really free?
Yes — every program is free, always. That's the founding promise.
Who are the tutors?
Volunteer undergraduate and graduate students of medical and biomedical engineering, plus practicing engineers for our speaker series.
Where do you operate?
Tutoring is online everywhere; labs and demo days run in person where our volunteers are, and virtually with mailed kits anywhere.
