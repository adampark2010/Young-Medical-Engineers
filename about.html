About us

Built by the curious, for the curious.

Young Medical Engineers provides free tutoring, hands-on lab activities, and in-person demonstrations that introduce students of every background to medical engineering — with real mentorship and practical experience, long before college.

We believe the next generation of healthcare innovators is already sitting in classrooms everywhere. Our work is simply to hand them the tools.

i. Access

Every program is free. Cost should never decide who gets to discover this field — sign-up is the only requirement.

ii. Craft

Real builds, not worksheets. Stethoscopes, prosthetic hands, and heart-rate sensors made by student hands.

iii. Mentorship

Tutors drawn from biomedical engineering programs and BMES chapters — people a few steps ahead, reaching back.
